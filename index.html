Before you go

Essential Tips

🎟️
Pre-book these immediately
DisneySea, TeamLab Borderless, USJ + Super Nintendo World area entry. All sell out weeks ahead in August. Book the moment you confirm flights.
💴
Money & payments
Get a Suica/Pasmo IC card at the airport — use it for all trains and convenience stores. Japan is still largely cash-based; bring ¥50,000 per person per week.
🚄
Tokyo → Kyoto train
Buy individual Hikari Shinkansen tickets (¥14,720 each) rather than a JR Pass — for this itinerary the Pass doesn't pay off. Book reserved seats in advance.
📱
Apps to download
Tokyo Disney Resort app (for Premier Access), USJ Official app (for Nintendo World entry tickets), Google Maps (works perfectly in Japan), HyperDia for trains.
🌡️
August weather
Expect 32–36°C and high humidity. Carry a small towel, fan and sunscreen. Tokyo feels hotter due to urban heat. Temples in the morning are cooler.
🧳
Luggage forwarding
Use Yamato Transport's "Takkyubin" service to forward luggage from Tokyo hotel to Kyoto hotel overnight (¥1,500–2,000/bag). Travel light on the Shinkansen.
